Explanation

This is a student project by students at NTNU Trondheim built in automn of year two

The project focuses on development process and scrum. Together with this site is a lot of documentation, and most notably an extensive group report as well as a personal report. A's and B's where received as a result

The project is built with html, css, bootstrap, js, python/django with PostgreSQL as database and published on heroku

Link to report/documentation

Project

The task of the project was to make an internal portal for information and personnel management for a fictional event company for the planning and doing of concerts and festivals

Functions thus include management for scheduling concerts and venues, technicians and artists.
